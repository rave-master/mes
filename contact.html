Frequently Asked Questions

To request a travel arrangement, please fill out the contact form with your details and requirements. Alternatively, you can email us directly at travel@company.com with your travel dates, destination, and purpose of travel. Our team will process your request within 24-48 hours.

For schedule changes, please submit your request at least 72 hours before your planned departure. Include your original travel details and the requested changes. Our team will assess the availability and cost implications before confirming your new schedule.

To claim reimbursement for travel expenses, submit all original receipts along with the completed expense claim form within 7 days of your return. Make sure to categorize expenses according to the provided guidelines. Reimbursements are typically processed within 14 business days.

International travel requires approval from your department head and the executive committee. Requests should be submitted at least 30 days in advance, with a detailed justification for the trip and expected outcomes. The travel department will assist with visa requirements and travel arrangements once approval is granted.
